About me

As a UX Designer/Engineer with a background in Information Science, I break down ambiguous problems systematically and come up with creative solutions. At Juniper Networks, now part of HPE, my work focused on making enterprise networking products easier to learn and adopt. Working at the intersection of design and engineering allows me to build experiences that are both practical and delightful to use.

I graduated from the University of Michigan with a master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction. Before that, I grew up in Taiwan and received my bachelor's degree from the Department of Library and Information Science at National Taiwan University.

When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me taking to the skies, enjoying comforting home-cooked meals, or collecting postcards from my travels.
